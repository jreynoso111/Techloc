Our Mission

Building a trusted network for every fleet.

TechLoc connects fleet operators with vetted field partners who keep vehicles moving. From installations to roadside emergencies, our team curates the right experts, verifies credentials, and delivers live coordination so every dispatch is quick, safe, and transparent.

Verified expertise

Each partner is reviewed for certifications, insurance, and service quality before joining the network.

Rapid response

Live coordination and real-time updates keep dispatchers and drivers aligned on every incident.

Nationwide reach

From metropolitan hubs to rural corridors, TechLoc delivers coverage wherever fleets operate.

Leadership

Dedicated to service quality

Our operations team blends fleet management veterans, logistics coordinators, and technology specialists. Together they refine the SOPs, training, and data quality that allow fleets to dispatch confidently.

Support

Always-on assistance
